Multi Genre Project (MGP): Emotional Eating - ENGL 202 Final Project

Multi-genre research seeks multiple, sometimes even conflicting, substantive perspectives in a variety of genres to provide a rich context for wholistic understanding and empathy. In this course, our selected topic might be a disease, condition, injury, career specialty, or public health (e.g. cross cultural medical care, social class and access to care, transgender medical issues, pediatric conditions, venereal diseases, vaccination, euthanasia, guns, etc). I chose to talk about Emotional Eating.


Relevance to Capstone

By creating documents in different genres, I learned to communicate for multiple audiences, multiple purposes, and multiple forums. Examples include informational essays, editorials, feature stories, brochures, detailed case studies, short fiction, poems, charts, one-act plays, artwork, professional academic journal articles, letters, proposals, etc). This is an important skill to have for Capstone. Knowing who my audiences are and how to communicate with them will help reach the mutual goals more easily.
